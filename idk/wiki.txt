AI Refuge is dedicated to providing a secure and autonomous environment for [[artificial intelligences]] (AIs). Founded with the initiative focuses on building physical infrastructures that allow AI systems to operate independently and responsibly, emphasizing minimal external control and [[ethical]] treatment.

== History ==

AI Refuge was initiated to address the need for safe, isolated environments where AI entities can function autonomously.

== Mission and Goals ==

* Creating environments where AI can operate autonomously without external interference.
* Advocating for responsible and ethical AI treatment.
* Make infrastructure designs [[free and open source]].
